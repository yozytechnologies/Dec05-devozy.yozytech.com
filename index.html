Challenges !!!

In digital era, any new application development needs a proper DEVOPS system to deploy/ship the software weekly, daily or multiple times a day. But it takes time to setup a proper DEVOPS system and need expertise. There are many tools available in the market and selection of tools is a challenge in this case. Hence the Project team end up building & maintaining a DEVOPS system rather than focusing the actual development

Typical challenges Enterprises faces, while building a DEVOPS system:

  • Choice of tools and Shortage of tools knowledge
  • Lack of knowledge to integrate tools
  • Build and Manage the Continues integration environments & CICD pipeline
  • Demands specialized team of DEVOPS engineers
  • Duplicate environments and lack of re-usability
  • Dependency with Deployment team for managing the DEVOPS system
  • Increase in project cost in turn affects ROI
  • Turnaround time to spin-up a new test environment
  • Multiple admin console & login accounts
